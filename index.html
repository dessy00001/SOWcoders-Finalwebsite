Rural School Funding

Home Aim How can you help About

Many rural schools in Belize are unequipped with the proper supplies and devices needed. This problem is due to the lack of proper government funding.

Promoting funding of the more unknown schools will have a positive impact on the community by helping and assisting parents in the community with supplies and devices that the school should be distributing. People should be aware of this problem because there are a lot of rural schools that are struggling even more than before amidst the pandemic.
